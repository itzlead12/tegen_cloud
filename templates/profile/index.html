What is TEGEN-CLOUD?

"Tegen (ተገን)" represents guidance, reference, and protection—reflecting our role as a trusted digital support system

The Problem

TFGBV through digital channels is increasing. Survivors face evidence deletion, manipulation, unsafe storage, and lack of authentication mechanisms. Current tools fail local realities.

Our Solution

A secure evidence guidance system with encrypted storage, cryptographic integrity proof, AI-assisted analysis, and survivor safety features—designed for Ethiopian context.

Impact

Strengthens digital justice by providing court-ready evidence with verifiable integrity, supporting NGOs, legal advocates, and survivors in seeking justice.
